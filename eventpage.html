------ About EDUINNOVATE'2025-------


"EduInnovate 2025 is the definitive global summit revolutionizing education through technology. This premier annual gathering hosted in London from July 10-12 will convene over 5,000 forward-thinking educators, policymakers, EdTech entrepreneurs, and learning scientists from 60+ countries. Under our 2025 theme 'Intelligent Learning Ecosystems', we'll pioneer actionable strategies for AI integration, immersive classrooms, and equitable digital access, bridging cutting-edge innovation with real-world implementation. Join UNESCO-endorsed workshops, hands-on tech demos, and groundbreaking research unveilings that will redefine how the world learns."
